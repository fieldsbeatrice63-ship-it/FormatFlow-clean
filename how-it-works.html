How It Works

Create professional documents in minutes with a simple, structured process.

1. Choose your document type

Select the type of document you want to create — resume, essay, business document, legal-style document, or eBook.

2. Choose a template

Select a template that matches your document style so your content is formatted correctly.

3. Add your content

Upload a file, paste your text, or type directly into the editor. FormatFlow prepares your content professionally.

4. Generate and refine

Improve wording, structure, and clarity using built-in tools before finalizing your document.

5. Preview your document

Review your formatted document in preview mode before choosing your access option.

6. Secure access and download

Choose your plan and proceed through secure checkout to access your completed document.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does unlimited standard use mean?

Unlimited standard use means you can create documents as needed for normal personal or professional use.

Can I upload my own document?

Yes. You can upload your file and have it professionally formatted or rewritten.

Can I create legal documents?

Yes. Business plans are best for contracts, agreements, and formal legal-style documents.

Can I create eBooks and essays?

Yes. Unlimited plans are best for long-form writing like eBooks, essays, and repeated content creation.

Will my final document include the watermark?

No. Final documents are clean and ready for use.

Which plan should I choose?

Single for one-time use, Pro for occasional use, Unlimited for high-volume writing, and Business for legal and client-facing documents.
